heritage trees

Year 2025
Category Concept · abstract
Role Art direction, visual exploration
Tools Illustrator, Photoshop
01 · the brief

the brief

Heritage Trees is a personal project: translating the memory and rootedness of remarkable trees into an abstract visual language, without literal illustration.

02 · the approach

the approach

Organic forms reduced to their structure: rings, branching, strata — evoking deep time without ever illustrating a literal tree.

03 · the result

the result

A series of abstract compositions, coherent with one another, forming a small, self-contained graphic language.

04 · deliverables

deliverables

A poster series, a sheet of reusable patterns, and documentation of the conceptual process.
