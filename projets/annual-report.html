annual report

Year 2025
Category Editorial
Role Editorial design (freelance)
Tools InDesign, Photoshop
01 · the brief

the brief

A real freelance client, a real annual report: shaping a year of activity into a dense editorial object that's both rigorous and enjoyable to read.

02 · the approach

the approach

Building a strict editorial grid able to absorb data, quotes, and visuals without losing readability.

Careful attention to typographic hierarchy to guide readers through a document few people read cover to cover.

03 · the result

the result

An annual report delivered on time, approved by the client, and now the base template for future editions.

04 · deliverables

deliverables

Complete InDesign layout, print files, and a reusable template for future editions of the report.
