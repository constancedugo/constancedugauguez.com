kinnect

Year 2026
Category Identity · app · cards
Role Art direction, problem solving
Tools Illustrator, Figma, InDesign
01 · the brief

the brief

Kinnect needed an identity able to carry an app, printed materials, and a range of physical cards — without losing focus.

The challenge: building a system flexible enough to live across multiple formats while staying instantly recognizable.

02 · the approach

the approach

The work started from a problem-solving mindset: simplifying a complex card system into a visual grammar that's clear, adaptable, and built to last.

Every decision — color, shape, typography — answers a real usage constraint, from screen to business-card format.

03 · the result

the result

A modular identity, designed for both the app and print, with a clear style guide that's easy to maintain over time.

04 · deliverables

deliverables

Complete style guide, app templates, card system, and usage guidelines for internal teams.
